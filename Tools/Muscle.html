MUSCLE CARS

Where raw power meets iconic design, muscle cars deliver an unparalleled driving thrill. With their potent engines and dynamic handling, they awaken the senses and dominate the asphalt with unmatched finesse. Representing the epitome of automotive performance, they embody speed, style, and the sheer joy of the open road.

Brand: Dodge

Model: Challenger SRT

Year: 2008 - Present

Engine: 6.2L supercharged HEMI V8

Cost: $60,000 to $90,000+

Performance: Straight-line speed and acceleration

     A quintessential American muscle car that commands respect with its bold presence and ferocious HEMI V8 engine. With its unmistakable retro-inspired design and tire-shredding performance, the Challenger SRT embodies the spirit of raw power and adrenaline-fueled excitement. Whether on the drag strip or cruising the streets, this iconic muscle car delivers an unrivaled driving experience that stirs the soul and leaves a lasting impression wherever it roams.

Brand: Ford

Model: Mustang

Year: Introduced in 1964, various iterations up to present

Engine: Turbocharged four-cylinder, V6 and V8

Cost: $30,000 to $70,000+

Performance: Strong acceleration, iconic muscle car styling

     An enduring symbol of American muscle, blending timeless design with exhilarating performance and a legendary V8 engine. With its iconic silhouette and thrilling power delivery, the Mustang embodies the spirit of freedom and adventure on the open road. Whether roaring down the highway or conquering the racetrack, every drive in a Mustang is a celebration of automotive heritage and the pursuit of driving excellence.

Brand: Pontiac

Model: GTO

Year: 1964 - 1974, later revived on 2004 - 2006

Engine: 6.4L V8 in classic, LS1 or LS2 V8 - new

Cost: $20,000 to $40,000+

Performance: Muscular styling, Impressive straight-line speed

     A classic American legend that commands attention with its iconic design and formidable V8 powerplant. With its timeless muscle car styling and exhilarating performance, the GTO symbolizes the golden era of American automotive ingenuity. Whether tearing up the streets or cruising the boulevard, every moment behind the wheel of a GTO is a nostalgic journey back to the glory days of pure, unbridled horsepower and unmatched driving excitement.

Brand: Shelby

Model: GT350

Year: 1965 - Present

Engine: 5.2L Voodoo V8 in recent models

Cost: $50,000 to $70,000+

Performance: Renowned for its track-focused performance

     A true American icon, meticulously crafted for exhilarating performance and timeless style. With its legendary V8 engine, precision handling, and distinctive Shelby design cues, the GT350 delivers an unforgettable driving experience that pays homage to its racing heritage. From its aggressive stance to its thrilling exhaust.

Brand: Ford

Model: Thunderbird

Year: 1955 - 2005

Engine: V8 in classics and V6 in later generations

Cost: $10,000 to $30,000+

Performance: Designed as a luxury cruiser

     A classic symbol of American automotive innovation, blending timeless design with luxurious comfort and powerful performance. With its iconic silhouette and refined interior, the Thunderbird embodies the essence of 1950s and 1960s cruising culture. Whether gliding down the highway or parked at a roadside diner, every journey in a Thunderbird is a nostalgic tribute to the golden age of American motoring, where style and sophistication reigned supreme.
